About Me

I am Thomas Hodgetts, and I have been building computers and playing games for most of my teenage to adult life. It became my dream to become a programmer, especially in the video games industry.

I have now graduated from Staffordshire University with a 1st in Computer Games Programming and a Distinction in Masters of Games Programming (by negotiated study). Over the course of my degree I have developed a passion for graphics and engine programming which I enjoy to work on in my spare time. I am also dabling in some audio programming. I am now persuing a role in the Video Games Industry as that is my dream industry and I hope to grow myself as a programmer from it.
