A Space Shooter using Unity and C#

Retreding and relearing old skills as well as new ones

To go where many men have gone before

When preparing for my second year at university I decided to brush up on some C# and Unity as for one I would be studying C# in the coming year and I wished to also start some small game development projects with my friends.

To begin the process of gaining a footing in unity I decided to take on a project that would have a simple design but also allowed for some complexity. For this I choose to follow a online course I had enrolled in before university that showed how to build a space shooter.
